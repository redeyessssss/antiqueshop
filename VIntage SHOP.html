FAQ: Bidding & Selling

Your vintage questions, answered

Wondering how to bid or sell your vintage finds? We’ve got you covered with all the details you need to join our friendly community and start your journey.

How do I place a bid?

Find an item you love, enter your bid above the base price, and confirm. We’ll notify you if someone outbids you, so you always have a chance to win.

How do I list my vintage item?

Create your account, upload clear photos, set your base price, and write a description. Once approved, your item will be ready for bids from fellow vintage fans.

What happens when I win a bid?

You’ll get an email with payment steps. After you pay, the seller ships your item straight to you—watch for tracking updates in your inbox.

Can I cancel my bid or listing?

Bids are final to keep things fair. If you need to remove a listing, just reach out to our support team and we’ll help you through the process.

How are payments and shipping handled?

Payments are safe and secure on our platform. Sellers handle shipping, and you’ll get tracking info as soon as your item is on its way.

Is there a fee for selling?

We charge a small commission per sale to keep things running smoothly. You’ll see the fee details before you finish your listing.
